Ilocos Empanada

Image by carmudi.com.ph

Ilocos Empanada is a fried meal with veggies or papaya, skinless Vigan Longganisa, and egg that is orange-tinged. It's just a rice wrapper packed with various stuff that's deep-fried in very hot oil until crisp, then cooled for a few minutes. After chipping off the corner to reveal the steaming-hot contents inside, the devouring begins. This is my favorite food because I love the crunchiness and perfectly paired with papaya and logganisa

Apex Legends

Image by Respawn Entertainment

Apex Legends is Respawn Entertainment's award-winning free-to-play Hero Shooter. In the next iteration of Hero Shooter and Battle Royale, master an ever-growing roster of legendary characters with tremendous powers, and experience smart squad play and unique gameplay. Apex Legends is an online multiplayer battle royale game in which three players form a team and use pre-made characters with unique abilities known as "Legends," similar to those found in hero shooters. Since the game's debut, alternate modes have been added that allow for single and two-player squads. This is my favorite game because I love how thrilling this game is and it is a fast phase game.
