How to get there:

A. By Air:
From Manila, you may fly to either Cauayan City Airport or Tuguegarao City Airport via Cebu Pacific. Flight to Cauayan Airport is once a day, while flights to Tuguegarao are available twice a day. Travel time from Tuguegarao City to Isabela is about 1 hour and 30 minutes by land.

B. By Land:
From Pasay, Cubao or Sampaloc, you may take air-conditioned buses like Victory Liner, Florida and 5 Star. These bus companies travel to major towns and cities in Isabela for 500-600 pesos one way. Travel time is about 8-10 hours depending on the stops. Victory Liner and Florida offer daily trips from Manila.
